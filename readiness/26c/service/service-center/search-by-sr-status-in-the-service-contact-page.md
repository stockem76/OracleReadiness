# Search by SR Status in the Service Contact Page

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/service/26c/bsvc-26c/26C-b2b-service-wn-f47304.htm)

In the Service Contact Page, Customer Service Representatives can search by status in the Recent Service Requests table.

Search by Status

It enables faster case resolution by helping service reps quickly find and prioritize service requests by status.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
