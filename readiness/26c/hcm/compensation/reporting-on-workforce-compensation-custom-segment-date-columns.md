# Reporting on Workforce Compensation Custom Segment Date Columns

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/comp-26c/26C-compensation-wn-f49860.htm)

Now you can broaden the coverage of rows displayed in the Workforce Compensation Real Time subject areas. We have included 6 new date columns in the existing folders 'Custom Columns' and 'Custom Columns for Worksheet Manager'. The following columns have been added in both the subject areas:

• Compensation - Workforce Compensation Real Time
• Compensation - Workforce Compensation Budgets Real Time

1. Date Column 1
2. Date Column 2
3. Date Column 3
4. Date Column 4
5. Date Column 5
6. Date Column 6

Extend your ability to report on data in the Compensation - Workforce Compensation Real Time subject areas. Allows users to view data for custom segment data columns.

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
