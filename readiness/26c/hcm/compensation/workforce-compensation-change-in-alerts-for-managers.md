# Workforce Compensation Change in Alerts for Managers

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/comp-26c/26C-compensation-wn-f45854.htm)

Alert options shown to managers in a Workforce Compensation plan have been streamlined to Error, Warning or Information.

Panel Drawer With New Alerts

Here is how the alerts have been migrated to the new types.

Migration of Alerts to New Types

Old Alert | New Alert
Error preventing save | Error
Error preventing submit | Error
Warning | Warning
Alert | Info
jiraWarning high | Info
Warning medium | Info
Warning low | Info
Information | Info
Square symbol | Info
Semicircle symbol | Info
Circle symbol | Info
Diamond symbol | Info
Triangle symbol | Info
Bar symbol | Info
Gear symbol | Info
Star 1 | Info
Star 2 | Info
Person | Info
Crossbars | Info

This change should reduce manager confusion regarding alerts. Managers can focus on alerts requiring action first.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

The Redwood Award Worksheet needs to be enabled for this change to be seen.

## 💡 Tips and Considerations

The ability to choose from the streamlined alert options when configuring will be available in a future release.
