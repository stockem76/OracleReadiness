# Associate Upto 25 Job Subfamilies with a Redwood Job Family

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/hure-26c/26C-hr-wn-f44417.htm)

You can now associate up to 25 job subfamilies with a job family in Redwood. After 25 job subfamilies are associated, the **+Add** button is disabled in the **Job subfamily associations** section of the job family page.

Associate up to 25 job subfamilies with a job family in Redwood

The maximum limit of the number of job subfamilies that can be associated with a job family has been increased from 10 to 25.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
