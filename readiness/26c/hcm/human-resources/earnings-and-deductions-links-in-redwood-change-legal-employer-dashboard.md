# Earnings and Deductions Links in Redwood Change Legal Employer Dashboard

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/hure-26c/26C-hr-wn-f49815.htm)

The Redwood Change Legal Employer Dashboard now includes a direct Earnings and Deductions link for applicable calculation card and element entry messages in the process results panel. The link opens the Redwood Earnings and Deductions page, where payroll-related entries are presented together in one experience.

  Additionally, security filtering has been updated so that if a user doesn't have access to the employee's Earnings and Deductions records, the related message and link aren't shown.

Messages on the Change Legal Employer page

Hyperlink to go to the Earnings and Deductions page

This feature reduces navigation after a legal employer change by letting users move from a process-result message to the payroll-related entries that need review.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
