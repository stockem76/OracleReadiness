# Prepare for Discontinuation of Support for OPA Integration in Journeys from Update 26D

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/hure-26c/26C-hr-wn-f43753.htm)

Oracle announced the end-of-life of Process Automation for Fusion Applications on October 31, 2025. New instance provisioning was disabled with this announcement. The service will reach end-of-life on October 31, 2026. After this date, support for Oracle Process Automation (OPA) integration in journeys will be discontinued.

Proactively evaluating and adopting alternate solutions ensures uninterrupted service and minimizes operational risk.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.

## 💡 Tips and Considerations

• You must evaluate alternate solutions using existing HCM Journeys features, such as the External URL task type or other non-journey solutions to avoid any service interruption.
