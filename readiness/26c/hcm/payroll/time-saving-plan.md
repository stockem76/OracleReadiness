# Time Saving Plan

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/payr-26c/26C-payroll-wn-f49631.htm)

France Payroll time saving plan (Compte Epargne Temps - CET) support is now available. Now you can:

• Transfer days from Vacation or RTT plans into a time saving plan.
• Take time saving plan days and process payment at current salary rate.
• Process termination payouts for remaining time saving plan balances.
• Display time saving plan-related balances and payment lines on payslip outputs.

Make use of this feature to configure and manage your time saving plan.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

• Configure and use a CET legislative grouping code for time saving plan absences.
• Create required customer absence types under CET so employees can take CET days in self service.
• Configure required payroll elements for delivered CET payment and deduction processing.
