# Time Card Approval Enhancements

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/tila-26c/26C-time-labor-wn-f49637.htm)

**Mass Approve Time Cards Enhancement**

Mass approval from the Redwood **Team Time Cards** page is now processed inline, when managers select and approve one or several time cards for approval. An in-progress indicator still appears in the status column during processing, with the final status reflected upon completion and a page refresh.

In-progress status indicator

**Approval History Enhancement**

Approval history information is improved by eliminating misleading contents such as **Withdrawn by Application**. The approval history now reflects a clear sequence of approval actions, including approver assignment, reassignment to Time and Labor manager in case of approval overrides, where applicable, and the final approval.

Time card approval history

Improved approval experience with enriched approval history enables mass approval and increased auditability for Time and Labor managers.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.

## 💡 Tips and Considerations

The approval history improvement is aligned whether the approval is done from the **Team Time Cards** page or the **Mass Submit and Approve Time Cards** process.
