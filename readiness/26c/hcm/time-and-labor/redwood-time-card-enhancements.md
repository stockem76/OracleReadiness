# Redwood Time Card Enhancements

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/tila-26c/26C-time-labor-wn-f49655.htm)

**Support of Entries Ending at Midnight Without Midnight Crossing Option Enabled**

Users can enter time entries that end exactly at midnight even when the **Enable time to span midnight** layout option is disabled, enabling workers to enter time until midnight (without crossing midnight). Entries spanning midnight remains dependent on the layout property 'Enable time to span midnight' enabled. This update applies to both time entries as well as change request flows.

**Pending Change Requests Banner Messaging Enhancements**

The pending change-requests banner on Redwood Time Cards now includes richer context providing the row number and the day of the associated time entry. Additionally, **View Change Requests** link enables direct navigation to the appropriate change requests pages, redirecting managers to the Team Change Requests page, and workers to the Change Requests page.

Pending change request banner seen by the worker

Improves time entry efficiency for users and enriched information for reviewing change requests directly from the time cards.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
