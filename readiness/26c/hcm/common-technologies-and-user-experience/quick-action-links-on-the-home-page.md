# Quick action links on the home page

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/common/26c/common26c/26C-common-wn-f49337.htm)

In Structure, when editing a quick action, the checkbox label for adding the quick action to the quick actions list on the home page has changed. It's now displayed as **Show as quick action link on home** instead of **Add as a quick action link on home page**.

Adding quick actions to home helps users initiate tasks more quickly.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
