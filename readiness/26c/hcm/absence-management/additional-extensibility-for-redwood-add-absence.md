# Additional Extensibility for Redwood Add Absence

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/amg-26c/26C-absence-mgmt-wn-f49549.htm)

You can now use Visual Builder studios to further extend the Redwood absence entry page.

With this release, you can:

• Add guided journeys to any region on the page, including new support for Other Details, Legislative Information, and Additional Information.
• Conditionally display guided journeys depending on the user's role - worker, manager, or administrator. For example, you can provide guided journeys to workers, but omit them when a manager or administrator is entering an absence on behalf of an employee.
• Set default values and validations based on specific attributes of the worker for whom the absence is being recorded, such as Legal Employer, Business Unit, and Country. For instance, you can default the planned end date for maternity leave to 6 months after the start date for US employees, and 12 months after the start date for UK employees.
• Show or hide buttons based on user roles. For example, the withdraw button is only available to workers, ensuring that administrators cannot withdraw an absence submitted by an employee.

Extend the enhanced Redwood Absence entry page which now supports guided journeys, setting up of default values, and allows to show or hide buttons based on user roles.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
