# Redwood: Timestamp Support for Talent Review Meeting Date Attributes in HCM Data Loader and HCM Extracts

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/tama-26c/26C-talent-mgmt-wn-f49444.htm)

From this release, you can specify the meeting data and ratings submission date as timestamps when you upload Talent Review meeting data. You can specify the timestamps using these attributes:

• DataSubmitDate
• MeetingDate

Similarly, when you extract Talent Review’s HRR_EXT_MEETINGS user entity, the meeting date and ratings submission date are extracted as timestamps.

## 🎯 Business Benefit

By using timestamps instead of just dates, ensure better accuracy and eliminate ambiguity.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
