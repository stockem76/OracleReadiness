# Redwood: VB Studio Page Properties for Granular AI Assist Enablement in Evaluation Topic Summaries

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/tama-26c/26C-talent-mgmt-wn-f47317.htm)

As an HR administrator, you can configure AI Assist comments separately by role and by comment type on Redwood performance documents. This lets you control whether AI Assist is available for individual item comments, section summary comments, overall summary comments, or all evaluation comments for managers, workers, and participants so you can support the needs of specific performance documents.

**Business benefit:**This feature enables you to configure AI Assist more precisely for managers, workers, and participants based on the type of evaluation comment you want to support.
