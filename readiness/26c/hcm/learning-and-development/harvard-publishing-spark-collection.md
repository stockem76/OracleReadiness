# Harvard Publishing Spark Collection

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/hcm/26c/lear-26c/26C-learning-wn-f50111.htm)

Import Harvard Business Impact: HBR Spark content into Oracle Learning as self-paced learning. You can keep your training catalog up to date by automating regular content refreshes from Harvard Business Impact: HBR Spark. The specific HBR Spark content available for import depends on your subscription plan and which content you can access from HBR Spark.

**Track Progress Seamlessly**

  Oracle Learning records each learner’s progress and completion as they work through HBR Spark content. When team members sign in to HBR Spark with their Oracle HCM work email, HBR Spark sends progress and completion details back using the xAPI protocol. This two-way integration means Oracle Learning shows one complete transcript for every user—even when learning happens on HBR Spark.

**Find HBR Spark Content Easily**

  Learners can quickly discover HBR Spark learning in the Oracle Learning catalog using the Publisher filter.

Searching the Learning Catalog by Publisher

**Manage Content in Redwood**

  Catalog managers have direct access to imported Harvard Business Impact: HBR Spark content through the new Redwood Self-Paced Learning page, which highlights key information about each learning.

Learning Catalog Page with Learning Filtered by Publisher

This enhancement makes more external content available in Oracle Learning, giving learners a single, easy-to-access catalog where they can find everything they need for their training in one convenient place.

## 💡 Tips and Considerations

• Harvard Business Impact manages its own content, so some details remain read-only in Oracle Learning. Content updates flow from Harvard Business Impact to Oracle Learning whenever changes are made.
• Now that Harvard has 2 providers, here is the key difference between them: 
  • **Harvard Business Impact (Harvard Business Publishing)** – includes products from the ***Harvard Business Publishing Collection*** and ***Harvard ManageMentor***
• **Harvard Business Impact (HBR Spark)** – Includes products from the ***HBR Spark*** and ***Harvard ManageMentor***
