# Redwood Theme For Enhanced Workbench

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/logistics/26c/otm26c/26C-otm-wn-f49885.htm)

This feature incorporates several of the Redwood style elements into the Enhanced Workbench. The enhancements provided include the following:

1. The Redwood strip added at the top of the Enhanced Workbench layouts.
2. Updating the color on the side navigation bar to the Redwood color scheme.

Enhanced Workbench - Redwood Theme Improvements

Below is a side-by-side view of the updated and improved Redwood theme verses the previous color theme.

Updated Redwood Enhanced Workbench Look vs. Original

**Business Benefit:** This feature extends the clean modern look and feel of the Redwood UI to the Enhanced Workbench, providing you with a consistent UI look and feel throughout the application.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.
