# Enhancement to Restricted Party Screening Workbench

`⚙️ Steps to Enable`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/logistics/26c/gtm26c/26C-gtm-wn-f49353.htm)

This feature introduces enhancements to the Restricted Party Screening Workbench.

• The "Passed" and "Failed" buttons for the RPLS status have been updated to display colors: 
  • Passed now appears in green
• Failed now appears in red
• Additionally, new buttons are available for the RPLS_OVERRIDE status values (left to right): 
  • Trusted Party
• Requires Screening
• Prohibited Party

Restricted Party Screening Workbench

**Business Benefit:**This enhancement improves the usability and efficiency of the Restricted Party Screening Workbench by:

• Making status indicators more visually intuitive and easier to interpret
• Enabling users to quickly designate parties as prohibited or trusted

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.

## 📚 Key Resources

For more information on the RPLS_OVERRIDE status type, refer to the Set Trusted or Prohibited Party for Restricted Party Screening topic in this document.
