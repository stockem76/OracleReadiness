# AI Agent Studio - Seeded Business Objects

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/logistics/26c/gtm26c/26C-gtm-wn-f49755.htm)

This feature provides you with a set of thirty pre-seeded OTM/GTM business objects that are available within the AI Agent Studio to simplify the development of your AI Agents.

Seeded Business Objects | 
Access Code | Order Movement
Bulk Plan | Order Release Line
Contact | Order Release
Cost Type | Payment Method Code
Document | Rate Offering
Document Type | Rate Record
Equipment | Remark Qualifier
Invoice | Sell Shipment
Involved Party Qualifier | Shipment Equipment
Item | Service Provider Alias Qualifier
Item Structure | Service Provider
Item With Product Classification | Shipment
Itinerary | Shipment Ship Unit
Location | Tender
Order Base | Trade

**Business Benefit:**The available pre-seeded business objects allow you start building your AI Agent utilizing the existing pre-seeded business objects instead of starting from scratch.  With the pre-seeded business objects you avoid:

• manual schema mapping
• REST endpoint discover
• payload construction
• object relationship modeling.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.

## 💡 Tips and Considerations

Additional business objects will be added in upcoming releases.
