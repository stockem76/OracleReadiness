# Redwood: Communicate Purchase Agreements to Multiple Email Recipients

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations` · `🔐 Access Requirements`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/scm/26c/proc26c/26C-procurement-wn-f46385.htm)

Add multiple email recipients when communicating purchase agreements to suppliers.

You can now notify internal stakeholders and additional supplier contacts through the same email sent when a new purchase agreement or change order is issued.

Communicate Agreements to Multiple Emails

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

If you haven't enabled Redwood for Purchasing, see the Getting Started with Redwood in Purchasing topic on the SCM Resource Center in Customer Connect.

## 💡 Tips and Considerations

• When the email field is defaulted from the supplier site, all email addresses configured for email communication at the supplier site are automatically populated in the email field.
• You can also provide multiple emails when uploading agreements using file-based data import.

## 🔐 Access Requirements

No specific privileges are needed to add multiple emails when editing a purchase agreement.

## 📚 Key Resources

For details on how the application defaults the email field, refer to the How Purchasing Document Header Defaults Work topic.
