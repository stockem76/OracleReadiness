# Redwood: View Additional Information About Location and Supplier

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations` · `🔐 Access Requirements`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/scm/26c/proc26c/26C-procurement-wn-f46352.htm)

View additional information about supplier, ship-to location, bill-to location, and deliver-to location when viewing purchase orders and purchase agreements using a Redwood page.

These screenshots illustrate the feature.

Bill-to Location Details on the View Agreement Page

Ship-to Location Details on the View Purchase Order Page

Deliver-to Location Details on the View Purchase Order Page

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

If you haven't enabled Redwood for Purchasing, see the Getting Started with Redwood in Purchasing topic on the SCM Resource Center in Customer Connect.

## 💡 Tips and Considerations

The supplier information drawer and location details aren't available on the Edit Purchase Order, Edit Purchase Agreement, and Edit Change Order pages.

## 🔐 Access Requirements

No specific privileges are needed to view the supplier information drawer and location details on purchasing documents.
