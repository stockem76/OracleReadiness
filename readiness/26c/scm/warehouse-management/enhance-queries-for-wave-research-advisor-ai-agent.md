# Enhance Queries for Wave Research Advisor AI Agent

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/logistics/26c/wms26c/26C-wms-wn-f50053.htm)

The **Wave Research Advisor** AI Agent now supports additional wave-related operational inquiries, enabling warehouse personnel to obtain more detailed allocation information through natural language interactions. Building on the capabilities introduced in 26A, the enhanced agent can answer questions about orders allocated to a specific wave and identify the IBLPNs associated with a wave run. These enhancements provide deeper visibility into wave execution and allocation activity, helping supervisors and managers investigate wave outcomes more quickly without navigating multiple application screens.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

No additional configuration is required beyond the Wave Research Advisor AI Agent setup introduced in 26A. Customers who have already configured and enabled the agent can immediately take advantage of the expanded query capabilities.
