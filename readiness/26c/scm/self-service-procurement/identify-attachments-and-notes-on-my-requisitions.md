# Identify Attachments and Notes on My Requisitions

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `💡 Tips & Considerations`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/scm/26c/ssproc26c/26C-ssproc-wn-f49452.htm)

In update 26B, we introduced the Identify Attachments and Notes on Requisition Lines feature, which displays an icon next to requisition lines with notes or attachments in the shopping cart and on the Requisition Line Details page. In this update, we’re extending this capability to the My Requisitions page.

Notes and Attachments on My Requisitions Page (Lines Tab)

Notes and Attachments on My Requisitions Page (Requisitions Tab)

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.

## 💡 Tips and Considerations

1. The icon will only be visible on the `Requisitions` tab if the requisition has a note or attachment at the requisition header level.
2. On the `Lines` tab, 
  1. the icon will be visible for any line that has a note or an attachment.
2. if there is a note or an attachment both on a line and the requisition header, the icon will only be visible on the corresponding line.
