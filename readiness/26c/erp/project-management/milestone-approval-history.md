# Milestone Approval History

`⚙️ Steps to Enable` · `🔐 Access Requirements`

> 📖 [View on Oracle Help Center](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/readiness/erp/26c/ppm26c/26C-ppm-wn-f49302.htm)

Enable project managers to view a detailed approval history for each milestone, including status changes, initiators, approvers, and timestamps for all approval actions. A clear record of the milestone approval events offers transparency and audit.

Milestone Status - Change History

The business benefit of this feature is to support operational compliance, as accurate approver logs help meet audit and regulatory requirements.

## ⚙️ Steps to Enable and Configure

You don't need to do anything to enable this feature.

## 🔐 Access Requirements

No new access requirements.
